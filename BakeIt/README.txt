This is an irc bot written for entertainment purposes. In it, multiple users bake food into a single shared table of food that is to be eaten (removed) upon request.

Each food item has a may be delicious, mediocre, trash, poisoned, or inaccessible. If not specified, each probability is equally likely if the food is not cake. Cake is always inaccessible unless specified otherwise.

.bake: bakes a food and adds it to the database
.nom or .eat: consumes a food to remove it from the database
